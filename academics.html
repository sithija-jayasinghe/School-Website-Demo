Academics at Nalanda

Building strong foundations and guiding students to national academic excellence.

Our Academic Approach

At Nalanda College, education is designed to nurture wisdom, discipline, and balanced growth. From Grade 1 to Advanced Levels, students receive a holistic education that prepares them for life, careers, and service to the nation.

Primary Section (Grades 1–5)

The Primary Section lays the foundation of learning. Students engage in subjects like:

  • Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies
  • Arts & Aesthetics
  • Social and Moral Education

Secondary Section (Grades 6–13)

The Secondary Section is divided into three stages:

  • Grades 6–9 – General curriculum with broad subject exposure
  • Grades 10–11 – Preparation for G.C.E. Ordinary Level (O/L) Examination
  • Grades 12–13 – Advanced Level (A/L) preparation with subject streams

G.C.E. Ordinary Level (O/L)

At the end of Grade 11, students sit for the national O/L examination. This stage focuses on core subjects including:

  • Sinhala / English / Tamil
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • History
  • Information Technology
  • Religion & Ethics
  • Health Education
  • Commerce
  • Arts & Aesthetics

G.C.E. Advanced Level (A/L)

In Grades 12 and 13, students choose a specialized academic stream leading to the national A/L examination. Streams offered at Nalanda include:

  • Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Technology
  • Commerce

Facilities Supporting Academics

  • Library with thousands of books and digital resources
  • Science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • IT labs with high-speed internet
  • Lecture halls and multimedia classrooms
