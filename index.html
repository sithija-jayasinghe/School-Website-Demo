Welcome to Nalanda College

- Character illumines Wisdom -

A Legacy of Wisdom and Excellence.

Since 1925, Nalanda College has stood as a beacon of learning, shaping generations of young minds with discipline, knowledge, and compassion. Rooted in Buddhist values yet open to the world, the College fosters intellectual growth, character development, and cultural harmony. With a proud heritage and an enduring spirit of innovation, Nalanda continues to guide its students towards leadership, service, and the pursuit of excellence.

Our Vision

To Gift the world great humans who are endowed with virtues and wisdom

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that nurtures intellectual, moral, and physical development, empowering students to become responsible citizens and compassionate leaders.

Academics

Education at Nalanda College goes beyond textbooks and examinations, nurturing balanced individuals enriched with wisdom and virtue. Guided by dedicated teachers and supported with facilities to meet diverse learning needs, the College aspires to shape students who are not only academically accomplished but also committed to uplifting the nation and serving humanity.

Clubs & Socities

Nalanda College celebrates the values of knowledge, discipline, and creativity that guide its students. The College offers a vibrant range of clubs, societies, and associations, giving Nalandians the opportunity to discover their passions and refine their talents. It fosters an environment where innovation and leadership thrive, empowering students to create communities of their own. This is the home of inspired minds and noble hearts determined to serve the nation and the world.

Sports

Carrying the maroon and silver with pride, the sportsmen of Nalanda College are molded to embody resilience, discipline, and respect. On the field, they learn the spirit of humility in triumph and dignity in defeat. Through teamwork, dedication, and the challenges they endure, Nalandians are forged into true sportsmen who rise above limits and inspire greatness.
