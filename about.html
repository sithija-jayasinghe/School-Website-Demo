About Nalanda College

Shaping generations with wisdom, virtue, and vision since 1925.

Our Story

Founded in 1925, Nalanda College has been a beacon of education in Sri Lanka, nurturing balanced individuals enriched with knowledge, discipline, and humanity. Over the years, our college has become a symbol of cultural pride and academic excellence.

Motto

“අපදාන සෝහිනී පඤඤා” | “Apadana Sobhini Pangna” | Wisdom Illuminates Character

Our Vision

“To Gift the world great humans who are endowed with virtues and wisdom.”

Our Mission

“To reward mother Lanka with noble sons with balanced personality dedicated to serve the country, nation and religion.”

Principal’s Message

At Nalanda, we believe in cultivating not just scholars but compassionate citizens. Our focus remains on discipline, academic brilliance, and spiritual growth — shaping leaders for tomorrow.

Our Faculties

The dedicated staff of Nalanda College are committed to guiding students both in and out of the classroom. With expertise in diverse fields, they ensure every Nalandian receives holistic education.

Our Journey Through Time

1925

Foundation

Nalanda College was founded to provide a strong educational foundation with Buddhist values.

1950s

Expansion

The college grew with new facilities, science labs, and sports infrastructure.

1970s

Achievements

Students of Nalanda excelled nationally in academics, sports, and culture.

2000s

Modern Era

Technology-driven classrooms and digital learning were introduced for holistic education.

Today

Continuing Legacy

Nalanda College stands as a symbol of excellence, producing leaders with wisdom and virtue.
